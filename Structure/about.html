Introduction

Bissan Specialized Industrial Company is a leading manufacturer of pipes and plastic components made from PVC material. With many years of experience in this vital sector, the company is committed to the highest standards of quality and safety. It strives to deliver advanced solutions that meet the needs of both local and regional markets in infrastructure, water supply, sewage systems, industrial applications, and electrical conduits. Through a specialized team and advanced production technologies, Bissan continues to strengthen its position as a trusted provider of high-performance plastic products that combine durability, efficiency, and innovation.

Why Us?

At Bissan Specialized Industrial Company, we believe that quality is not an option—it is the foundation of everything we do. We stand out through:

  • High quality and precise standards in manufacturing PVC pipes and plastic components.
  • Extensive experience in infrastructure, development, and industrial sectors.
  • Advanced production technologies ensuring durability and efficiency.
  • Strong commitment to deadlines and consistent service quality.
  • A specialized team delivering innovative solutions.
  • A professional workforce focused on optimal client solutions.

Choosing Bissan means choosing trust, quality, and sustainability.

Vision

To be the leading regional company in manufacturing PVC pipes and plastic components, recognized for high quality, sustainable innovation, and effective contribution to infrastructure development.

Mission

To provide high-quality PVC products through advanced manufacturing, continuous innovation, and commitment to customer satisfaction, while supporting sustainable development and infrastructure growth.
